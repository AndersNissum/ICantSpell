Welcome to ICantSpell

ICantSpell lets you dictate text by voice — hold a key, speak, and your words appear wherever you're typing, in any app.

Everything runs locally on your Mac. No internet connection. No cloud service. Your voice never leaves your device.

Accessibility Permission

ICantSpell needs Accessibility permission to inject your dictated text into any app — word processors, browsers, text editors, and more. Without it, voice typing will not work.

Click "Grant Accessibility" to open System Settings. Add ICantSpell to the Accessibility list, then return to this window.

Microphone Permission

ICantSpell needs Microphone permission to capture your voice while you hold the push-to-talk key. There is no background listening — the microphone is active only while the key is held.

Click "Grant Microphone" to allow access. If the system dialog does not appear, click it again to open System Settings.

Push-to-Talk Hotkey

Choose the key you'll hold to start dictating. Most people use Right Option — it doesn't interfere with normal typing.

Click the field below, then press any key or combination.
